Guillotine Automatic Multi-leaf Vertical Sliding Windows & Doors

The EXPansion minimalist system and solutions has been designed to a level of quality, versatility and reliability until now unattainable.

BES offer standard and bespoke automatic guillotine (Vertical) window & doors comprises of two or three vertically sliding leafs. The frames are concealed in the finishes with 21 mm minimal visible leaf frame to expand your view. EXP G guillotine automatic multi-leaf vertical sliding windows & doors can open upward or downward.

Features

minimal design

21 mm wide slim aluminium profile.

Vision

Up to 6 meter high & 3 meter wide sliding leaf size.

Glazing

Single, double & triple glass up to 54 mm thick.

Multitrack

Security

Combining a versatile sliding system with multi-rail options the EXP-C Standard system delivers all modern architectural aesthetic and technical requirements – utilizing high mechanical capacity with the option of sequential motorized openings of several sheets.

Creating contemporary bespoke solutions, using the highest quality products, we deliver exceptional quality minimal sliding windows and doors that provide both maximum comfort and timeless appeal.

Features of Our Minimal Sliding Doors & Windows are:

minimal design

21 mm wide slim aluminium profile.

Vision

Up to 6 meter high & 3 meter wide sliding leaf size.

Glazing

Single, double & triple glass up to 54 mm thick.

Durability

Up to 1000 Kg leaf running on stainless steel wheels & guide rails

Multitrack

Visible & concealed multitrack frame to allow for endless applications.

Security

Multipoint locking system for additional safety & performance.

Thermal Insulation

U Value 0.8 – 1.7 (W/m²K).

Acoustic

Up to 41 DB.

Motorized

Automatically operated sliding doors .

Bespoke Solutions

We make an architects dream a reality. Our multi-track system provides endless options that can be either manual & motorized.
